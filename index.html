About Us

Am happy to introduce Ourselves RJS Sea foods , Traders from India – concentrating only on the Frozen Sea Food Products.

We have been in this industry for the last 15 years, we have very good networks in sea food products, presently we have our presence in both the East and west coast of India. RJS holds a wide knowledge and network for frozen sea foods on Shrimps (Vannamei and Black tiger) Cephalopods (Squid, Cuttlefish & Octopus) & Fish Products. RJS is helping buyers better source seafood products by the market information with best sourcing.

We take care of the complete transaction of seafood imports for our customers, which includes -market information, sourcing of right products , price negotiation, contracts, packing material designing and follow-ups. Production supervision, which includes the raw material control, pre-processing control, processing control, packing and labelling, inspection , shipment monitoring and report, documents etc. We have our own team for Quality control. We look forward for your valuable enquiries to work on the quotation.
