About Us

We stand for our reputation and “customer service” is “our motto”.

We are buying agents for Frozen Sea foods from India. Our Company was established in the year 2013 & we have vast knowledge and wide network for Frozen Sea Foods. (Frozen Shrimps, Cephalapods & Fish Products.)

We take care of the complete transaction of seafood imports for our customers, which includes -market information, sourcing of right products , price negotiation, contracts, packing material designing and follow-ups. Production supervision, which includes the raw material control, pre-processing control, processing control, packing and labelling, inspection , shipment monitoring and report, documents etc. We have our own team for Quality control. We look forward for your valuable enquiries to work on the quotation.
